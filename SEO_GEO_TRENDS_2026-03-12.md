## Key trend signals (2026)
1. **GEO + SEO convergence is now the default model**
   - Teams are moving from “rankings only” to dual visibility (SERP + AI answers).
2. **Answer-first formatting is outperforming long intros**
   - Content with direct 40–60 word summary blocks is more frequently cited.
3. **Citation authority > pure position-based thinking**
   - Mentions on trusted third-party sites increasingly influence AI retrieval confidence.
4. **Visibility tracking is expanding to LLM KPIs**
   - Citation share, mention quality, and retrieval coverage are becoming practical metrics.
5. **Freshness cadence matters more**
   - High-intent pages now need tighter refresh loops (30–60 days) for competitive queries.
