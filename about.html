My Testing Philosophy

I test every tool I review in my actual business for a minimum of 30 days. Usually longer. I track time savings, output quality, and ROI. I only write about tools I have direct, substantive experience with.

I don't accept sponsorships, free products in exchange for reviews, or affiliate arrangements that influence what I recommend. If I have any financial relationship with a company I mention, I'll disclose it explicitly. This policy is non-negotiable.

My Review Standards

  • Minimum 30 days of real business use before reviewing
  • Track quantitative results (time saved, cost, output quality)
  • No sponsorships or undisclosed financial arrangements
  • Update reviews when tools change significantly
  • Include both pros and genuine cons ‚Äî nothing is perfect
