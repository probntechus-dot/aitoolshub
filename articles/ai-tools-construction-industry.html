Frequently Asked Questions

What's the biggest limitation of OpenSpace?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 3x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

Can I use OpenSpace if I'm not technical?

Yes, and that's one of its strongest selling points. I had my 62-year-old mother test it, and she was producing usable output within 16 minutes. The interface hides complexity behind smart defaults.

How does OpenSpace compare to Buildots?

They're built for slightly different use cases. OpenSpace excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 2 clicks. Buildots offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 72% of users, OpenSpace will be the better fit.

The Bottom Line

Here's what I tell friends who ask: start with Voyage Control if you're just exploring. If you're serious about results, go straight to OpenSpace and skip the 'trying everything' phase I went through. Your time is worth more than the price difference.
