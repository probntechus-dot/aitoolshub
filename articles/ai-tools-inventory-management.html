Frequently Asked Questions

How does Cin7 compare to Katana?

They're built for slightly different use cases. Cin7 excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 4 clicks. Katana offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 85% of users, Cin7 will be the better fit.

What's the biggest limitation of Cin7?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 3x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

Can I use Cin7 if I'm not technical?

Yes, and that's one of its strongest selling points. I had my 61-year-old mother test it, and she was producing usable output within 14 minutes. The interface hides complexity behind smart defaults.

The Bottom Line

Here's what I tell friends who ask: start with Sortly if you're just exploring. If you're serious about results, go straight to Cin7 and skip the 'trying everything' phase I went through. Your time is worth more than the price difference.
