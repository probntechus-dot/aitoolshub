Why AI Pricing Is More Confusing Than It Should Be

AI tools use at least four different pricing models, sometimes simultaneously on the same platform:

  • Flat subscriptions ‚Äî fixed monthly fee for a set of features (ChatGPT Plus at $20/month)
  • Usage-based/credit systems ‚Äî pay per word generated, image created, or token used (Midjourney credits)
  • Seat-based ‚Äî per user per month, scales with team size (Jasper Teams, Zapier Teams)
  • Operation/task-based ‚Äî pay per automation run regardless of team size (Make.com, Zapier tasks)
