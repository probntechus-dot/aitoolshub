Top Tools

1. Salesforce Einstein

Predicts deal close probability, revenue forecast. Cost: $75-150/user/month. Accuracy: 85-90% on deals >$10K.

2. HubSpot Sales Forecasting

Simple, built-in forecasting. Good for SMBs. Cost: $45-100/user/month. Accuracy: 75-85%.

3. Clari (Advanced)

Deep pipeline analysis, AI coaching for reps. Cost: $500+/month. Accuracy: 90%+.
