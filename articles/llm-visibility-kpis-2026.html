LLM Visibility KPIs: How to Measure GEO Performance in 2026

Mar 12, 2026·⏱ 8 min read

Most teams still report only keyword rankings. In 2026, that misses half the picture. You also need LLM visibility metrics.

Core GEO KPIs to track

1) Citation Share

How often your brand/page appears in AI answers for target prompts.

2) Mention Quality

Are you mentioned as “recommended,” “alternative,” or “low-cost option”? Positioning matters as much as frequency.

3) Retrieval Coverage

Across your target topic map, what percent of sub-queries have at least one page that can be cited?

4) Assisted Conversion Rate

Users who discover your brand via AI often convert later through branded search, direct, or email. Track multi-touch impact.

5) Freshness Velocity

How quickly important pages are updated after tool/pricing/product changes.

Simple dashboard for founders

  • 20 high-value prompts
  • Weekly citation check across major engines
  • Monthly content refresh score
  • Quarterly topic gap audit

What “good” looks like after 90 days

  • Citation Share up 20–40%
  • Top pages updated at least every 45 days
  • At least 3 supporting pages per core topic
  • Improved branded search + direct traffic trendline
