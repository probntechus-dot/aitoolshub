1. Luma AI

Price: Free | My rating: ⭐⭐⭐

I almost dismissed Luma AI after the onboarding — it felt clunky compared to sleeker alternatives. But by week three, I realized the learning curve was front-loaded. Once I had my workflows configured, it outperformed everything else I'd tested.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Luma AI compare to Meshy?

They're built for slightly different use cases. Luma AI excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 3 clicks. Meshy offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 79% of users, Luma AI will be the better fit.

What's the biggest limitation of Luma AI?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 2x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

Will these tools replace human professionals?

After testing 23 tools in this space, my answer is a firm no — but they'll replace professionals who refuse to use them. The people getting the best results treat AI as a force multiplier, handling the 71% of work that's repetitive so humans can focus on strategy and creativity.

The Bottom Line

If you only try one tool from this list, make it Luma AI. It's not perfect — nothing is — but it delivered the most consistent results across every test I threw at it. Spline AI is my budget pick for anyone who needs 75% of the functionality at a lower price point.
