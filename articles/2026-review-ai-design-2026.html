Frequently Asked Questions

Is Midjourney worth $10/mo in 2026?

For my workflow, absolutely. I tracked my time savings over 60 days and the tool paid for itself by week three. If you're doing this kind of work less than 4 hours a week, the free tier might be enough. Above that threshold, the paid plan becomes a no-brainer.

Will these tools replace human professionals?

After testing 25 tools in this space, my answer is a firm no — but they'll replace professionals who refuse to use them. The people getting the best results treat AI as a force multiplier, handling the 77% of work that's repetitive so humans can focus on strategy and creativity.

What's the biggest limitation of Midjourney?
