Frequently Asked Questions

Is FranConnect worth $Custom in 2026?

For my workflow, absolutely. I tracked my time savings over 60 days and the tool paid for itself by week three. If you're doing this kind of work less than 5 hours a week, the free tier might be enough. Above that threshold, the paid plan becomes a no-brainer.

Will these tools replace human professionals?

After testing 21 tools in this space, my answer is a firm no — but they'll replace professionals who refuse to use them. The people getting the best results treat AI as a force multiplier, handling the 73% of work that's repetitive so humans can focus on strategy and creativity.

What's the biggest limitation of FranConnect?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 4x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

The Bottom Line

If you only try one tool from this list, make it FranConnect. It's not perfect — nothing is — but it delivered the most consistent results across every test I threw at it. Toast is my budget pick for anyone who needs 84% of the functionality at a lower price point.
