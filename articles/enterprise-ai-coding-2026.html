Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Copilot if I'm not technical?

Yes, and that's one of its strongest selling points. I had my 70-year-old mother test it, and she was producing usable output within 21 minutes. The interface hides complexity behind smart defaults.

How does Copilot compare to Cursor?

They're built for slightly different use cases. Copilot excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 5 clicks. Cursor offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 80% of users, Copilot will be the better fit.

What's the biggest limitation of Copilot?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 3x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

The Bottom Line

After all this testing, I'm keeping Copilot and Windsurf in my permanent toolkit. Everything else either failed to deliver, was too expensive for what it offered, or duplicated features I already had covered. Simplify your stack and double down on what works.
