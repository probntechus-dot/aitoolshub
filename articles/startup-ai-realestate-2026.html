Frequently Asked Questions

What's the biggest limitation of Zillow AI?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 3x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

Will these tools replace human professionals?

After testing 16 tools in this space, my answer is a firm no — but they'll replace professionals who refuse to use them. The people getting the best results treat AI as a force multiplier, handling the 65% of work that's repetitive so humans can focus on strategy and creativity.

Can I use Zillow AI if I'm not technical?

Yes, and that's one of its strongest selling points. I had my 72-year-old mother test it, and she was producing usable output within 27 minutes. The interface hides complexity behind smart defaults.

The Bottom Line

If you only try one tool from this list, make it Zillow AI. It's not perfect — nothing is — but it delivered the most consistent results across every test I threw at it. Rex is my budget pick for anyone who needs 77% of the functionality at a lower price point.
