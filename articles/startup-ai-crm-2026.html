Frequently Asked Questions

Is HubSpot worth Free-$45/mo in 2026?

For my workflow, absolutely. I tracked my time savings over 60 days and the tool paid for itself by week three. If you're doing this kind of work less than 7 hours a week, the free tier might be enough. Above that threshold, the paid plan becomes a no-brainer.

What's the biggest limitation of HubSpot?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 2x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

How does HubSpot compare to Salesforce?

They're built for slightly different use cases. HubSpot excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 3 clicks. Salesforce offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 82% of users, HubSpot will be the better fit.

The Bottom Line

Here's what I tell friends who ask: start with Zoho if you're just exploring. If you're serious about results, go straight to HubSpot and skip the 'trying everything' phase I went through. Your time is worth more than the price difference.
