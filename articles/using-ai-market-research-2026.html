Market Research Gets Fast

Traditional market research took months and cost $50K+. AI market research takes weeks and costs $5K. I tested tools that conduct surveys, analyze sentiment, identify trends.

Speed Advantage: Companies using AI research iterate faster. They test ideas, get feedback, pivot quickly. Competitors using traditional research are stuck.

Tools

1. SurveySparrow + AI Analysis

AI-powered surveys that adapt based on responses. Auto-analyze results. Cost: $50-200/month. Value: Actionable insights in days.

2. Google Trends + Claude

Use Google Trends to identify interest, Claude to analyze patterns. Cost: Free + $20. Value: Free insight into market direction.

3. Brandwatch + AI

Monitor social, news, reviews. AI identifies emerging trends before competitors. Cost: $500+/month. Value: Market awareness.
