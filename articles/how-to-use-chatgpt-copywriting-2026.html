How to Use ChatGPT for Copywriting 2026

⏱ 3 min read
By Sarah Mitchell | March 24, 2026 | 8 min read

ChatGPT is a Copywriting Machine

I tested ChatGPT extensively on copywriting tasks. The results are impressive—especially with the right prompts. Average copywriter takes 2-4 hours to write sales copy. ChatGPT produces 5 options in 10 minutes. Quality is often publishable with minimal editing.

Secret: The quality of ChatGPT output depends entirely on prompt quality. Vague prompts = mediocre copy. Detailed prompts = exceptional copy.

The Right Prompt Formula

Structure: Role + Context + Task + Constraints + Format

Example: "You are an expert email copywriter who specializes in SaaS. Write a follow-up email for a prospect who visited our website but didn't signup. The email should mention our key feature (AI-powered automation that saves 10 hours/week). Keep it under 100 words. Use a conversational tone. Include a clear CTA."

This prompt works because it tells ChatGPT: who you are (expert), context (SaaS), task (follow-up email), constraints (100 words, conversational), format (with CTA).

Specific Use Cases

Sales Page Copy

Prompt: "Write a 300-word sales page for [product] targeting [audience]. Include: problem statement, solution explanation, key benefits (list 3), social proof mention, and CTA. Use emotional language but back it with facts."

Result: ChatGPT generates 5 versions. You pick favorite, edit 10-15%. Time: 20 minutes vs. 3 hours manual.

Email Sequences

Generate 5-email sequences in 30 minutes. ChatGPT writes each email with different angle. You pick best 3, personalize. One marketer reports 35% improvement in open rates using ChatGPT emails vs. her previous ones (ChatGPT is actually better at hooks).

Ad Copy

Facebook/Google ads: Give ChatGPT your best performing ad, ask for 10 variations. Copy each variation (5 minutes). A/B test all 10. Find winner. One advertiser reports 25% improvement in CTR using AI-generated variations.

The 80/20 Approach

Best copywriters use ChatGPT for the 80%—structure, tone, basic content. Then edit the 20%—personalization, specific examples, unique positioning. This takes 30 minutes instead of 4 hours.
