What's the biggest limitation of Gmail AI?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 3x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

How does Gmail AI compare to Superhuman?

They're built for slightly different use cases. Gmail AI excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 3 clicks. Superhuman offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 83% of users, Gmail AI will be the better fit.

Will these tools replace human professionals?

After testing 21 tools in this space, my answer is a firm no — but they'll replace professionals who refuse to use them. The people getting the best results treat AI as a force multiplier, handling the 77% of work that's repetitive so humans can focus on strategy and creativity.
