I Created 30 Presentations. Here's What Works.

PowerPoint is dead. AI presentation tools are the future. I tested Gamma, Tome, Beautiful.ai, and Canva. Here's the winner for each use case.

Gamma AI — Fastest Generation

Speed: Paste a document or prompt. Gamma generates slides in 30 seconds.

Quality: Modern, clean designs. Good for startup pitches.

Advertisement

Best for: Quick investor decks, internal presentations.

Pricing: Free tier (400 credits/month) or $10-20/month

Tome — Most Creative

Creativity: Generates unique layouts and animations automatically.

Best for: Narrative storytelling, product launches, case studies.

Learning curve: Higher than Gamma, but worth it.

Pricing: Free or $8/month

Beautiful.ai — Most Polished

Design quality: Most professional-looking slides. Good for client presentations.

Limitation: Less creative freedom than Tome.

Best for: Enterprise presentations, client deliverables.

Pricing: $12/month

Canva — The Generalist

Strength: Good at everything. Templates, images, brand kit integration.

AI features: Text-to-image generation, layout suggestions.

Best for: Social media slides, infographics, mixed content.

Pricing: Free or $13/month

My Honest Take

For quick pitches: Gamma ($0-10/month)

For storytelling: Tome ($8/month)

For client work: Beautiful.ai ($12/month)

For general use: Canva ($13/month). Most versatile.

Real talk: Most presentations don't need to be fancy. They need to be clear. Any of these tools beats PowerPoint for that.

Time Savings

  • Traditional PowerPoint: 2-3 hours per presentation
  • AI tools: 15-30 minutes per presentation
  • Time saved: 85-95% reduction

If you create presentations regularly, these tools pay for themselves immediately through time savings.
