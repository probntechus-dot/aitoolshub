Frequently Asked Questions

How does Zoom compare to Google Meet?

They're built for slightly different use cases. Zoom excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 5 clicks. Google Meet offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 75% of users, Zoom will be the better fit.

Can I use Zoom if I'm not technical?

Yes, and that's one of its strongest selling points. I had my 69-year-old mother test it, and she was producing usable output within 24 minutes. The interface hides complexity behind smart defaults.

Will these tools replace human professionals?

After testing 19 tools in this space, my answer is a firm no — but they'll replace professionals who refuse to use them. The people getting the best results treat AI as a force multiplier, handling the 64% of work that's repetitive so humans can focus on strategy and creativity.

The Bottom Line

After all this testing, I'm keeping Zoom and Otter.ai in my permanent toolkit. Everything else either failed to deliver, was too expensive for what it offered, or duplicated features I already had covered. Simplify your stack and double down on what works.
