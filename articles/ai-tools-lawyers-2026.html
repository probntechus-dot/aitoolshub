The Tools That Made the Cut

1. Harvey AI

2. Clio AI

Price: $39/mo | My rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the biggest limitation of Harvey AI?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 4x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

How does Harvey AI compare to Clio AI?

They're built for slightly different use cases. Harvey AI excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 4 clicks. Clio AI offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 83% of users, Harvey AI will be the better fit.

Can I use Harvey AI if I'm not technical?

Yes, and that's one of its strongest selling points. I had my 59-year-old mother test it, and she was producing usable output within 24 minutes. The interface hides complexity behind smart defaults.

The Bottom Line

After all this testing, I'm keeping Harvey AI and LawGeex in my permanent toolkit. Everything else either failed to deliver, was too expensive for what it offered, or duplicated features I already had covered. Simplify your stack and double down on what works.
