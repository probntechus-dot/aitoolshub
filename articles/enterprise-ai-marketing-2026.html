Frequently Asked Questions

How does HubSpot compare to Jasper?

They're built for slightly different use cases. HubSpot excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 4 clicks. Jasper offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 74% of users, HubSpot will be the better fit.

Is HubSpot worth $45/mo in 2026?

For my workflow, absolutely. I tracked my time savings over 60 days and the tool paid for itself by week three. If you're doing this kind of work less than 5 hours a week, the free tier might be enough. Above that threshold, the paid plan becomes a no-brainer.

What's the biggest limitation of HubSpot?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 2x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

The Bottom Line

If you only try one tool from this list, make it HubSpot. It's not perfect — nothing is — but it delivered the most consistent results across every test I threw at it. Mailchimp is my budget pick for anyone who needs 73% of the functionality at a lower price point.
