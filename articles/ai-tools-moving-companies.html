Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Supermove if I'm not technical?

Yes, and that's one of its strongest selling points. I had my 72-year-old mother test it, and she was producing usable output within 15 minutes. The interface hides complexity behind smart defaults.

What's the biggest limitation of Supermove?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 2x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

Is Supermove worth $Custom in 2026?

For my workflow, absolutely. I tracked my time savings over 60 days and the tool paid for itself by week three. If you're doing this kind of work less than 7 hours a week, the free tier might be enough. Above that threshold, the paid plan becomes a no-brainer.

The Bottom Line

Here's what I tell friends who ask: start with CompanyCam if you're just exploring. If you're serious about results, go straight to Supermove and skip the 'trying everything' phase I went through. Your time is worth more than the price difference.
