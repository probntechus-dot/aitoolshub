Frequently Asked Questions

How does Notion compare to ClickUp?

They're built for slightly different use cases. Notion excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 4 clicks. ClickUp offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 75% of users, Notion will be the better fit.

Can I use Notion if I'm not technical?

Yes, and that's one of its strongest selling points. I had my 59-year-old mother test it, and she was producing usable output within 23 minutes. The interface hides complexity behind smart defaults.

Is Notion worth $10/mo in 2026?

For my workflow, absolutely. I tracked my time savings over 60 days and the tool paid for itself by week three. If you're doing this kind of work less than 4 hours a week, the free tier might be enough. Above that threshold, the paid plan becomes a no-brainer.

The Bottom Line

After all this testing, I'm keeping Notion and Coda in my permanent toolkit. Everything else either failed to deliver, was too expensive for what it offered, or duplicated features I already had covered. Simplify your stack and double down on what works.
