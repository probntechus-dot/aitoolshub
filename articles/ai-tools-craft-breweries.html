Frequently Asked Questions

What's the biggest limitation of Ekos?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 4x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

How does Ekos compare to Arryved?

They're built for slightly different use cases. Ekos excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 3 clicks. Arryved offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 82% of users, Ekos will be the better fit.

Can I use Ekos if I'm not technical?

Yes, and that's one of its strongest selling points. I had my 67-year-old mother test it, and she was producing usable output within 28 minutes. The interface hides complexity behind smart defaults.

The Bottom Line

Here's what I tell friends who ask: start with Untappd if you're just exploring. If you're serious about results, go straight to Ekos and skip the 'trying everything' phase I went through. Your time is worth more than the price difference.
