The AI Translation Revolution

Modern neural machine translation (NMT) has shattered the limits of earlier rule-based systems. Today's AI translation:

  • Understands context — disambiguates words with multiple meanings
  • Preserves tone — maintains formal/casual distinction
  • Handles domain-specific terminology — technical, legal, medical, financial
  • Processes documents — PDF, Word, Excel with formatting intact
  • Scans handwriting — OCR + translation for written documents

FAQ

Can AI handle industry-specific terminology in translations?

Yes, with caveats. DeepL and Google Translate handle common business terminology well (95%+ accuracy). Highly specialized domains (medical, legal, scientific) benefit from custom glossaries. DeepL Pro allows you to define specific terms, improving accuracy to near-human levels.

Is AI translation secure for confidential business documents?

DeepL and Google both commit not to store translated documents. However, for maximum security, use locally-hosted translation engines (open-source alternatives like Argos) or Unbabel's on-premise solutions. For non-confidential business documents, cloud translation services are fine.
