Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Shopify if I'm not technical?

Yes, and that's one of its strongest selling points. I had my 59-year-old mother test it, and she was producing usable output within 30 minutes. The interface hides complexity behind smart defaults.

Will these tools replace human professionals?

After testing 17 tools in this space, my answer is a firm no — but they'll replace professionals who refuse to use them. The people getting the best results treat AI as a force multiplier, handling the 75% of work that's repetitive so humans can focus on strategy and creativity.

Is Shopify worth $29/mo in 2026?

For my workflow, absolutely. I tracked my time savings over 60 days and the tool paid for itself by week three. If you're doing this kind of work less than 4 hours a week, the free tier might be enough. Above that threshold, the paid plan becomes a no-brainer.

The Bottom Line

If you only try one tool from this list, make it Shopify. It's not perfect — nothing is — but it delivered the most consistent results across every test I threw at it. Rebuy is my budget pick for anyone who needs 80% of the functionality at a lower price point.
