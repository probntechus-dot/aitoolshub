Frequently Asked Questions

Will these tools replace human professionals?

After testing 22 tools in this space, my answer is a firm no — but they'll replace professionals who refuse to use them. The people getting the best results treat AI as a force multiplier, handling the 67% of work that's repetitive so humans can focus on strategy and creativity.

How does Tableau compare to Power BI?

They're built for slightly different use cases. Tableau excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 3 clicks. Power BI offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 85% of users, Tableau will be the better fit.

What's the biggest limitation of Tableau?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 3x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

The Bottom Line

If you only try one tool from this list, make it Tableau. It's not perfect — nothing is — but it delivered the most consistent results across every test I threw at it. Amplitude is my budget pick for anyone who needs 78% of the functionality at a lower price point.
