Monday Morning: The Week Setup (30 minutes)

Every Monday I open ChatGPT and paste my calendar for the week plus any important notes from Friday. I ask it to: identify potential conflicts, flag anything I need to prepare for, and suggest what I should work on in my first 2-hour deep work block. This takes 5 minutes and sets me up better than any planner app I've tried.

Monday planning with ChatGPT takes 30 minutes and saves hours of wasted effort later in the week. Photo: Unsplash

Daily: Email Processing (Saves ~30 min/day)

I have a Zapier automation that drafts responses to inquiry emails overnight. Every morning, I have 8-12 Gmail drafts waiting. I review them, make minor edits, and hit send. What used to take 90 minutes now takes 20.

Tool: Zapier + ChatGPT + Gmail. Setup details in my Zapier AI automation guide.

After Every Meeting: Auto-Summary (Saves ~25 min/meeting)

I use Otter.ai on every call. When the call ends, the transcript goes automatically via Zapier to ChatGPT, which creates a summary with action items, then creates a Notion page and drafts a follow-up email. Zero manual effort. I just review and send.

Content Creation: 3x Faster (Saves ~2 hrs/week)

I write blog posts (like this one). Before AI: full post took 3-4 hours. Now: I write a detailed outline and key points (30 min), ChatGPT generates a structured first draft (2 min), I edit heavily (45-60 min). Total: about 1.5 hours instead of 4. Friday: Automated Weekly Report (Saves ~1 hr/week)

Every Friday at 4pm, a Zap pulls data from HubSpot (leads), QuickBooks (revenue), and my time tracker, sends everything to ChatGPT with a prompt to write a narrative summary, and emails it to me. I read it over coffee. The insight quality is good enough that I actually look forward to Friday afternoons now.
