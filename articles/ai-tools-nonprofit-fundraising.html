Frequently Asked Questions

What's the biggest limitation of Bloomerang?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 2x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

How does Bloomerang compare to DonorPerfect?

They're built for slightly different use cases. Bloomerang excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 2 clicks. DonorPerfect offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 83% of users, Bloomerang will be the better fit.

Is Bloomerang worth $125/mo in 2026?

For my workflow, absolutely. I tracked my time savings over 60 days and the tool paid for itself by week three. If you're doing this kind of work less than 6 hours a week, the free tier might be enough. Above that threshold, the paid plan becomes a no-brainer.

The Bottom Line

Here's what I tell friends who ask: start with GiveSmart if you're just exploring. If you're serious about results, go straight to Bloomerang and skip the 'trying everything' phase I went through. Your time is worth more than the price difference.
