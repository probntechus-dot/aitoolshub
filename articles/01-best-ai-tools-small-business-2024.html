ChatGPT Plus by OpenAI

ChatGPT Plus is probably already on your radar, and yeah ‚Äî it deserves to be. After three months, it's still the first tab I open every morning.

But here's the thing most people miss: ChatGPT isn't magic. It's more like having a very smart intern who needs clear instructions. When I first started using it, I'd give it vague prompts and get vague results. The moment I started writing detailed, specific prompts, everything changed.

Last month, I used it to write a 15-page proposal for a new client. Would have taken me two days. With ChatGPT doing the first draft? Four hours. And I landed the contract.
