Frequently Asked Questions

What's the biggest limitation of Midjourney?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 3x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

Can I use Midjourney if I'm not technical?

Yes, and that's one of its strongest selling points. I had my 56-year-old mother test it, and she was producing usable output within 24 minutes. The interface hides complexity behind smart defaults.

How does Midjourney compare to Canva AI?

They're built for slightly different use cases. Midjourney excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 3 clicks. Canva AI offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 83% of users, Midjourney will be the better fit.

The Bottom Line

After all this testing, I'm keeping Midjourney and Looka in my permanent toolkit. Everything else either failed to deliver, was too expensive for what it offered, or duplicated features I already had covered. Simplify your stack and double down on what works.
