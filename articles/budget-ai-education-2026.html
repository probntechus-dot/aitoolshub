Frequently Asked Questions

How does Duolingo compare to Khan Academy?

They're built for slightly different use cases. Duolingo excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 2 clicks. Khan Academy offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 83% of users, Duolingo will be the better fit.

What's the biggest limitation of Duolingo?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 3x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

Will these tools replace human professionals?

After testing 23 tools in this space, my answer is a firm no — but they'll replace professionals who refuse to use them. The people getting the best results treat AI as a force multiplier, handling the 68% of work that's repetitive so humans can focus on strategy and creativity.

The Bottom Line

If you only try one tool from this list, make it Duolingo. It's not perfect — nothing is — but it delivered the most consistent results across every test I threw at it. Notion is my budget pick for anyone who needs 72% of the functionality at a lower price point.
