Frequently Asked Questions

What's the biggest limitation of ActiveCampaign?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 4x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

How does ActiveCampaign compare to Klaviyo?

They're built for slightly different use cases. ActiveCampaign excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 4 clicks. Klaviyo offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 76% of users, ActiveCampaign will be the better fit.

Is ActiveCampaign worth $29/mo in 2026?

For my workflow, absolutely. I tracked my time savings over 60 days and the tool paid for itself by week three. If you're doing this kind of work less than 3 hours a week, the free tier might be enough. Above that threshold, the paid plan becomes a no-brainer.

The Bottom Line

If you only try one tool from this list, make it ActiveCampaign. It's not perfect — nothing is — but it delivered the most consistent results across every test I threw at it. Brevo is my budget pick for anyone who needs 73% of the functionality at a lower price point.
