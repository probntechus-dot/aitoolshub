Frequently Asked Questions

What's the biggest limitation of Monday.com?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 2x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

Is Monday.com worth $9/mo in 2026?

For my workflow, absolutely. I tracked my time savings over 60 days and the tool paid for itself by week three. If you're doing this kind of work less than 3 hours a week, the free tier might be enough. Above that threshold, the paid plan becomes a no-brainer.

How does Monday.com compare to Asana?

They're built for slightly different use cases. Monday.com excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 5 clicks. Asana offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 78% of users, Monday.com will be the better fit.

The Bottom Line

After all this testing, I'm keeping Monday.com and Wrike in my permanent toolkit. Everything else either failed to deliver, was too expensive for what it offered, or duplicated features I already had covered. Simplify your stack and double down on what works.
