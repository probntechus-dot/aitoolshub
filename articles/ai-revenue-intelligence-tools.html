How to Get Started with AI Sales Tools

Implementing AI into your sales workflow does not require a complete overhaul. Here is a proven approach:

  1. Identify your biggest time sink. Find the sales task consuming the most time with repetitive work.
  2. Start with a free trial. Test with real projects, not just demos.
  3. Measure before and after. Track output metrics to justify the investment.
  4. Scale gradually. Expand to additional workflows after validating one use case.
  5. Join the community. User communities accelerate learning with shared templates and best practices.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose between Gong AI and Clari?

It depends on your needs. Gong AI excels at speed and automation, ideal for high-volume sales work. Clari offers superior customization for teams focused on quality. Both offer free trials.

What integrations do these tools support?

Most support Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Slack, and popular CRMs. Gong AI has 200+ native integrations, while Clari provides deeper sales-specific connections.

How long to learn these AI Sales tools?

Basic proficiency takes 1-3 days. Advanced features require 2-4 weeks of regular use. Gong AI has the gentlest learning curve, while InsightSquared is most powerful but complex.

What is the best AI Sales tool for beginners in 2026?

For beginners, Gong AI offers the most intuitive interface with guided onboarding. The free tier provides enough functionality to evaluate whether AI sales tools fit your workflow before committing.
