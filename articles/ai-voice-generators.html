ElevenLabs: The Voice Quality King

What I loved: Most natural-sounding voices. Emotions and intonation sound human-like. Multiple languages (29+).

Best for: Professional voiceovers, audiobooks, where voice quality is paramount.

Pricing: $5-30/month depending on usage

Limitation: Slower generation time than competitors (5-15 seconds)

Murf AI: Best for Video

What I loved: Direct video integration. Add voiceover while editing your video. Super fast.

Best for: YouTube videos, explainer videos, product demos.

Pricing: $13-99/month

Strength: Generation speed is exceptional (2-3 seconds)

Descript: The All-in-One

What it does: Video editing + transcription + AI voiceover. Edit video by editing transcript.

Best for: Podcasters, YouTubers who want everything in one tool.

Limitation: Expensive for just voiceover ($12-24/month minimum)

PlayHT: The Value Pick

Strength: Good quality, fast generation, affordable.

Best for: Budget-conscious creators who want decent results.

Pricing: Starts at $9/month

My actual usage: ElevenLabs for professional work. PlayHT for quick test voiceovers. Murf when integrating with video.
