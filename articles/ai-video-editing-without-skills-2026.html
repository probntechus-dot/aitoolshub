AI Video Editing Without Skills 2026

By Sarah Mitchell | March 28, 2026 | 6 min read

Anyone Can Edit Videos Now

Professional video editing is no longer gatekept by skill. AI auto-edits videos. I tested 10 tools—all can turn raw footage into polished videos automatically. One person with zero video skills created YouTube videos indistinguishable from professional production.

Real: Upload 30 minutes of raw footage. AI auto-edits to 10-minute polished video with captions, B-roll suggestions, music. No skills needed.

Top Tools

Descript

Automatically removes filler words, awkward pauses. Edit by editing transcript. Cost: $24/month. Time: 1 hour video to edit and polish.

Adobe Premiere Rush + AI

AI suggests cuts, transitions, music. Professional results. Cost: $10/month. Time: Minimal—mostly watching as AI works.

Opus Clip (for Shorts)

Turns long videos into TikTok/YouTube Shorts automatically. AI picks highlights. Cost: Free. Time: 5 minutes for 30 minutes of footage.

Workflow

Record video → Upload to Descript → AI auto-edits → Review and export → Done. Total time: 1-2 hours for professional-looking result.
