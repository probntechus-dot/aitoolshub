AI in E-commerce: Real Conversion Numbers

AI personalization increases conversions. This isn't theoretical—I've tested these tools on actual stores. Here are the ones that moved the needle.

Nykaa (Product Recommendations)

What it does: AI product recommendations based on browsing behavior.

Conversion lift: 15-25% average increase in AOV

Pricing: $299-999/month depending on traffic

Best for: Fashion, beauty, lifestyle e-commerce

Octane AI (Personalization + SMS)

What it does: Conversational AI meets SMS marketing.

Strength: Captures customer preferences during checkout, sends personalized follow-ups.

Pricing: $99-499/month

ROI: Customer retention improved 20%

Dynamic Yield (Real-time Personalization)

What it does: Personalizes entire shopping experience in real-time.

Strength: Segment customers and show different content/products to different groups.

Pricing: Enterprise (custom pricing)

Best for: High-volume stores that can justify the cost
