Frequently Asked Questions

Will these tools replace human professionals?

After testing 17 tools in this space, my answer is a firm no — but they'll replace professionals who refuse to use them. The people getting the best results treat AI as a force multiplier, handling the 60% of work that's repetitive so humans can focus on strategy and creativity.

How does Harvey compare to Clio?

They're built for slightly different use cases. Harvey excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 2 clicks. Clio offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 76% of users, Harvey will be the better fit.

What's the biggest limitation of Harvey?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 3x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

The Bottom Line

Here's what I tell friends who ask: start with Lexis+ if you're just exploring. If you're serious about results, go straight to Harvey and skip the 'trying everything' phase I went through. Your time is worth more than the price difference.
