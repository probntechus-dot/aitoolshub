Frequently Asked Questions

What's the biggest limitation of Trainerize?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 4x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

Will these tools replace human professionals?

After testing 17 tools in this space, my answer is a firm no — but they'll replace professionals who refuse to use them. The people getting the best results treat AI as a force multiplier, handling the 66% of work that's repetitive so humans can focus on strategy and creativity.

Is Trainerize worth $5/mo in 2026?

For my workflow, absolutely. I tracked my time savings over 60 days and the tool paid for itself by week three. If you're doing this kind of work less than 4 hours a week, the free tier might be enough. Above that threshold, the paid plan becomes a no-brainer.

The Bottom Line

If you only try one tool from this list, make it Trainerize. It's not perfect — nothing is — but it delivered the most consistent results across every test I threw at it. Exercise.com is my budget pick for anyone who needs 85% of the functionality at a lower price point.
