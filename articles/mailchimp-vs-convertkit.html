Frequently Asked Questions

Will these tools replace human professionals?

After testing 25 tools in this space, my answer is a firm no — but they'll replace professionals who refuse to use them. The people getting the best results treat AI as a force multiplier, handling the 69% of work that's repetitive so humans can focus on strategy and creativity.

How does Mailchimp compare to ConvertKit?

They're built for slightly different use cases. Mailchimp excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 2 clicks. ConvertKit offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 72% of users, Mailchimp will be the better fit.

Can I use Mailchimp if I'm not technical?

Yes, and that's one of its strongest selling points. I had my 64-year-old mother test it, and she was producing usable output within 27 minutes. The interface hides complexity behind smart defaults.
