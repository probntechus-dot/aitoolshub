The Tools That Made the Cut

1. Runway

Price: $12/mo | My rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

I track my tools on a simple ROI spreadsheet. After 36 days with Runway, the numbers tell the story: $357 saved in outsourcing costs, 10 hours reclaimed, and zero missed deadlines attributable to the tool.

What impressed me:

  • Export options let me take my data if I ever want to switch

Frequently Asked Questions

Will these tools replace human professionals?

After testing 23 tools in this space, my answer is a firm no — but they'll replace professionals who refuse to use them. The people getting the best results treat AI as a force multiplier, handling the 67% of work that's repetitive so humans can focus on strategy and creativity.

What's the biggest limitation of Runway?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 2x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

How does Runway compare to Synthesia?

They're built for slightly different use cases. Runway excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 3 clicks. Synthesia offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 79% of users, Runway will be the better fit.

The Bottom Line

Here's what I tell friends who ask: start with Pika if you're just exploring. If you're serious about results, go straight to Runway and skip the 'trying everything' phase I went through. Your time is worth more than the price difference.
