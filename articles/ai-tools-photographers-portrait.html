Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Aftershoot if I'm not technical?

Yes, and that's one of its strongest selling points. I had my 63-year-old mother test it, and she was producing usable output within 13 minutes. The interface hides complexity behind smart defaults.

Is Aftershoot worth $15/mo in 2026?

For my workflow, absolutely. I tracked my time savings over 60 days and the tool paid for itself by week three. If you're doing this kind of work less than 5 hours a week, the free tier might be enough. Above that threshold, the paid plan becomes a no-brainer.

What's the biggest limitation of Aftershoot?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 3x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

The Bottom Line

If you only try one tool from this list, make it Aftershoot. It's not perfect — nothing is — but it delivered the most consistent results across every test I threw at it. Retouch4me is my budget pick for anyone who needs 76% of the functionality at a lower price point.
