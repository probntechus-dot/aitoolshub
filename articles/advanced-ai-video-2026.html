What's the biggest limitation of Runway?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 3x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

Will these tools replace human professionals?

After testing 20 tools in this space, my answer is a firm no — but they'll replace professionals who refuse to use them. The people getting the best results treat AI as a force multiplier, handling the 80% of work that's repetitive so humans can focus on strategy and creativity.

Can I use Runway if I'm not technical?

Yes, and that's one of its strongest selling points. I had my 59-year-old mother test it, and she was producing usable output within 11 minutes. The interface hides complexity behind smart defaults.
