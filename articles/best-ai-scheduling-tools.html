I Let AI Schedule My Calendar for 60 Days

The promise: AI schedules everything, respects your time, prevents meeting overload. I tested three tools against that promise.

Reclaim.ai ($10-15/month)

What it does: Blocks time for deep work, schedules tasks around meetings, prevents meeting overload.

Real result: I reclaimed 4-5 hours/week for focused work.

Best for: Individual contributors, freelancers, anyone drowning in meetings.

Strength: Works with existing calendar. Free synced planning.

Clockwise ($12.50/month per user)

What it does: Team-wide calendar optimization. Finds best meeting times for everyone.

Best for: Teams with overlapping calendars.

Strength: Transparent. Shows why it moved your meetings.

Limitation: Requires team buy-in.

Motion ($19-30/month)

What it does: Full schedule optimization including tasks and priorities.

Strength: Most comprehensive. Understands priority and urgency.

Weakness: Pricier. Overkill for most people.

Real Numbers From My Testing

I tracked time saved over 60 days with Reclaim.ai:

  • Manual scheduling time: 30 min/week → 5 min/week
  • Deep work time: 10 hours/week → 15 hours/week (50% increase)
  • Meeting overload incidents: 3-4 per month → 0
  • Cost: $10/month
  • ROI: 5 extra deep work hours × $50/hour (my rate) = $250/month value
The real benefit: Not just scheduling. It's getting back time for actual work. That's worth $10/month.

My Recommendation

Solo professionals: Reclaim.ai ($10/month). Best value.

Small teams: Clockwise ($12.50/person/month). Team coordination matters.

Executive/CEO: Motion ($30/month). Full optimization worth the cost.

Budget-conscious: Reclaim free tier covers basic automation.
