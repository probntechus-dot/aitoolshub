Frequently Asked Questions

How does Bench compare to Pilot?

They're built for slightly different use cases. Bench excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 3 clicks. Pilot offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 75% of users, Bench will be the better fit.

What's the biggest limitation of Bench?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 2x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

Is Bench worth $249/mo in 2026?

For my workflow, absolutely. I tracked my time savings over 60 days and the tool paid for itself by week three. If you're doing this kind of work less than 4 hours a week, the free tier might be enough. Above that threshold, the paid plan becomes a no-brainer.

The Bottom Line

Here's what I tell friends who ask: start with Zeni if you're just exploring. If you're serious about results, go straight to Bench and skip the 'trying everything' phase I went through. Your time is worth more than the price difference.
