Frequently Asked Questions

What's the biggest limitation of Teal?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 2x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

Is Teal worth $29/mo in 2026?

For my workflow, absolutely. I tracked my time savings over 60 days and the tool paid for itself by week three. If you're doing this kind of work less than 6 hours a week, the free tier might be enough. Above that threshold, the paid plan becomes a no-brainer.

How does Teal compare to Kickresume?

They're built for slightly different use cases. Teal excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 3 clicks. Kickresume offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 77% of users, Teal will be the better fit.

The Bottom Line

If you only try one tool from this list, make it Teal. It's not perfect — nothing is — but it delivered the most consistent results across every test I threw at it. Enhancv is my budget pick for anyone who needs 75% of the functionality at a lower price point.
