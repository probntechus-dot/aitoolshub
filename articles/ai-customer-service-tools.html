What are the best AI customer service tools for small business?

The best AI customer service tools for small business are Intercom Fin (best overall, $39/month + $0.99/resolution), Tidio AI with Lyro (best for budget-conscious businesses, $29/month with free plan), Freshdesk AI Freddy (best if already in Freshworks ecosystem), and Zendesk AI (best enterprise option, overkill for most small businesses).
