1. AI for Writing & Content

Writing is where solopreneurs feel the most pressure. Proposals, emails, blog posts, social media, client reports ‚Äî it never ends. AI cuts this workload by 70-80%.

Claude (Best for Long-Form Writing)

Cost: $20/month (Pro) | Free tier: Yes, limited

Claude is my primary writing tool. I use it for client proposals, blog posts, email sequences, and long-form reports. Its 200K context window means I can paste in an entire document and ask it to rewrite, summarize, or expand sections. The writing quality is consistently better than other models for nuanced business content.

My daily workflow: Every Monday, I paste my week's tasks into Claude and ask it to draft all emails, outlines, and first drafts simultaneously. What used to take 3 hours takes 30 minutes.

ChatGPT (Best for Research + Quick Tasks)

Cost: $20/month (Plus) | Free tier: Yes

I use ChatGPT alongside Claude for tasks where web browsing matters ‚Äî competitive research, checking current pricing, summarizing recent news. The combination of both ($40/month total) covers every writing need a solopreneur has.
