  3. Measure before and after. Track output metrics to justify the investment.
  4. Scale gradually. Expand to additional workflows after validating one use case.

How much do AI Marketing tools typically cost?

Current AI Marketing tools augment rather than replace humans. They automate repetitive tasks and process large data volumes, but human oversight remains essential for quality control and strategic decisions.
