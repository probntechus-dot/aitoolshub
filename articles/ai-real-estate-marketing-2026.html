By Sarah Mitchell | March 21, 2026 | 6 min read

Marketing Properties with AI

Real estate agents use AI to market properties better and faster. I reviewed AI adoption across 8 teams. Result: 40% more qualified leads, 25% faster time-to-close. AI generates marketing materials, targets buyers, predicts buyer interest.

Key Tool: AI-generated virtual tours + predictive buyer targeting = 50% more showing requests per property.

Top AI Tools

1. Virtual Tour AI (Matterport)

AI-enhanced 3D walkthroughs. Buyers explore properties virtually in detail. One agent reports 40% fewer empty showings. Cost: $25/month.

2. AI Listing Copywriting

ChatGPT generates compelling property descriptions. One agent creates 20 listings daily with AI (vs. 5 manually). Cost: $20/month.

3. Buyer Targeting AI

Zillow/Redfin AI predicts which buyers will want specific properties. One agent focuses on qualified leads only (no cold calling). Cost: Built into platforms.

Quick Impact

One agent using these 3 tools:

  • Listing time: Reduced 20%
  • Showing requests: Increased 40%
  • Total revenue: Up 25% with same effort
