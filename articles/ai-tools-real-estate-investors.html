Frequently Asked Questions

Will these tools replace human professionals?

After testing 16 tools in this space, my answer is a firm no — but they'll replace professionals who refuse to use them. The people getting the best results treat AI as a force multiplier, handling the 80% of work that's repetitive so humans can focus on strategy and creativity.

What's the biggest limitation of DealMachine?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 4x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

Can I use DealMachine if I'm not technical?

Yes, and that's one of its strongest selling points. I had my 63-year-old mother test it, and she was producing usable output within 18 minutes. The interface hides complexity behind smart defaults.

The Bottom Line

Here's what I tell friends who ask: start with RealtyMogul if you're just exploring. If you're serious about results, go straight to DealMachine and skip the 'trying everything' phase I went through. Your time is worth more than the price difference.
