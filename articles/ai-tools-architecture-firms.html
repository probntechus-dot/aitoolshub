Frequently Asked Questions

Will these tools replace human professionals?

After testing 22 tools in this space, my answer is a firm no — but they'll replace professionals who refuse to use them. The people getting the best results treat AI as a force multiplier, handling the 62% of work that's repetitive so humans can focus on strategy and creativity.

How does Autodesk AI compare to ArchiStar?

They're built for slightly different use cases. Autodesk AI excels at speed and simplicity — I could get results in 4 clicks. ArchiStar offers more customization but requires more setup time. For 80% of users, Autodesk AI will be the better fit.

What's the biggest limitation of Autodesk AI?

Honestly? The pricing tiers. The jump from the starter plan to the professional plan is steep — about 2x the cost — and several key features are locked behind that upgrade. I wish they offered a middle ground.

The Bottom Line

Here's what I tell friends who ask: start with Revit AI if you're just exploring. If you're serious about results, go straight to Autodesk AI and skip the 'trying everything' phase I went through. Your time is worth more than the price difference.
