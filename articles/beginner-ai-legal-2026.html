Can I use Harvey if I'm not technical?

Yes, and that's one of its strongest selling points. I had my 72-year-old mother test it, and she was producing usable output within 23 minutes. The interface hides complexity behind smart defaults.

Is Harvey worth $Custom in 2026?

For my workflow, absolutely. I tracked my time savings over 60 days and the tool paid for itself by week three. If you're doing this kind of work less than 3 hours a week, the free tier might be enough. Above that threshold, the paid plan becomes a no-brainer.
